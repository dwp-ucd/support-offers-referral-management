WHP Disability Condition overview

The Work and Health Programme (WHP) helps to break down barriers for people who need additional support to find work.

Working with local services, the WHP provides dedicated and flexible support for up to 15 months that focuses on:

  • learning new skills
  • building confidence
  • increasing work experience

An additional 6 months of in-work support is also available.

Taking part in the opportunity is voluntary unless the person has been unemployed for 24 months or more.

To be eligible the person must:

  • be disabled, as defined by the Equality Act 2010
  • be over 18 years old in England, or 16 years old in Wales
  • have the right to work in the UK
  • not be in any paid work, including zero hours contracts
  • not currently be on the WHP
  • not have been on the WHP before
  • not be on another national programme

To be suitable the person must:

  • be committed to the goal of finding work in 12 months
  • need additional support to find work
