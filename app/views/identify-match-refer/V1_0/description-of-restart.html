Restart Scheme overview

The Restart Scheme will give you extra support with finding jobs in your local area.

You’ll get personalised support for up to 12 months that will focus on:

  • developing your skills to boost your employability
  • helping break down barriers to working
! Warning You do have to take part in this programme or your benefits could stop.
