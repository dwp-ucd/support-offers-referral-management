Work and Health Programme overview

The Work and Health Programme (WHP) will give you additional support with finding a job.

It will help you to learn new skills, build your confidence and increase your experience with the support of local services. Your personal action plan will give you manageable steps to break down barriers to work and achieve your goal.

You can get dedicated and flexible support for up to 15 months. If you find a job within that time, you will also get 6 months in-work support.

Taking part in the programme is voluntary, unless you have been unemployed for 24 months or more.
