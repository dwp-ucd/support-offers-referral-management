Cancel this referral

In order to cancel this referral, you will need to select the appropriate reason for the citizen not being able to participate.

The reason you must select will depend on which programme the citizen was referred to.

For Universal Credit referrals to Restart

You will need to read paragraphs 2.47 to 2.49 of the provider guidance for Universal Credit Restart.

For income-based Jobseeker's Allowance referrals to Restart

You will need to read paragraphs 2.46 to 2.48 of the provider guidance for income-based Jobseeker's Allowance Restart.

For Access to Work referrals

You will need to read paragraph 4.10 of the provider guidance for Access to Work.

For Intensive Personalised Employment Support referrals

You will need to read paragraph 3.2 of the provider guidance for Intensive Personalised Employment Support.

For Work and Health referrals

You will need to read paragraphs 56 to 60 and 72 of the provider guidance for the Work and Health Programme.

Select the reason that matches the guidance for your programme:
